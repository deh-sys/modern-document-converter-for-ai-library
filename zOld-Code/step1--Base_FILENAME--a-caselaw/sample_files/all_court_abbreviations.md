## NOTES ON STATE TRIAL COURTS

**Important:** The above list includes only appellate courts (supreme courts and intermediate appellate courts). State trial courts (Superior Courts, Circuit Courts, District Courts, County Courts, Municipal Courts, etc.) are NOT comprehensively listed in the free resources available.

For state trial court abbreviations, consult:
- The official Bluebook (Table T.1)
- State-specific citation manuals
- Individual state court websites

**General Format for Trial Courts:**
Trial court abbreviations typically follow this pattern when needed in citations:
- [State Abbr.] [Court Type] Ct.
- Examples: "Cal. Super. Ct." (California Superior Court), "Fla. Cir. Ct." (Florida Circuit Court)

However, specific formatting varies by state and must be verified in official sources.
